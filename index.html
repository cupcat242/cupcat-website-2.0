Blockchain Game

We are planning to make a blockchain game, where all NFTs from Cupcats (Cupcats, Cupcat houses, and Cupcat Kittens) will be usable in game.

This game is planned to be played by EVERYONE, but only Cupcat holders will be able to play with their own Cupcats, houses, and kittens as long as they hold them. We already made a deal for this game, game will be made as centralized but will be connected to the blockchain.

By playing this game, Cupcat holders will be able to earn a gaming token, this token will have liquidity to buy & sell, Cupcat holders will be able to earn this token by playing the game, but a tax will be redacted from every sale to use for marketing purposes. Name of the token is yet to be revealed, we are still thinking about options.

More details about the gameplay will come after our meetings with our partner game company.

Cupcat Shop

As our community already knows, we are working for a shop that’s not only limited to Cupcat products.

Building a shop was the first big thing on our roadmap. Many people from our community didn’t agree with us, some left, some didn’t think physical products would be a success. Many of these thoughts are mostly because we couldn’t deliver the message well, so here are some details:

We are not looking to create a shop for Cupcat owners. Our target is creating products for every cat lover, with and without Cupcat designs. We are aiming to create a product line in many different areas:

  • Streetwear (T-shirts, sweatshirts, jackets etc.)
  • Home apparel (Pyjamas, bathrobes etc.)
  • Accessories (Hats, backpacks, patches etc.)
  • Plushies and pins
  • Mugs
  • Collectibles
  • ...and many more that we will announce on a later date.

All of the designs will be about cats, and every Cupcat holder will have a lifetime discount on the shop.

We are also working to make a cryptocurrency pay option. This may or may not become true, so no promises but if we can make this, we will build our shop on multiple chains to provide a better shopping experience.

cupkitten's Story

Hello everyone, I’m cupkitten. The creator of the Cupcats and the artist at the same time.

I’m from a developing country. People not living here might think this is a good place to live, a good holiday destination, but in reality I live in a place where women live in fear everyday.

We wake up to murders, missing women news, abuses and many more stories every day, women normally have rights on paper in my country, but we actually don’t in life. People who kill women mostly don't get sentenced, abuses are never punished, underage marriages are everywhere. I’m not from a place where hijab or burqa is a must, but women who are not wearing it are being judged in many places, and men think not wearing these is an invitation to abuse.

I live with my boyfriend (husband to be, his nickname is catfish) and we have 4 cats living with us, and we also pay expenses for 2 cats in my family home where I used to live. I also always help stray cats in our streets. I live in a place we can call a “cat neighborhood” and there are hundreds of cats here. I’m doing my best to feed them. My boyfriend didn’t have a bad life, he wasn’t rich but he also wasn’t poor, I learned many things while watching him sitting next to me and researching crypto everyday. He was trying to earn money with his investments so we can get rid of this country and live abroad.

Things went bad, he lost a lot of money because he tried things he didn’t know, and I know this is a classic in crypto. But we didn’t give up on our dreams, and that’s where I thought maybe I could try making an NFT to change our situation. He supported me when I moved in with him, bought my first graphic tablet. Even though I could work as a nurse, the profession I have a degree from college, he wanted me to follow my passion, and pushed me to become better at something I never found support for.

I wanted to leave this country, I wanted to save my life, I wanted to save my cats’ and boyfriend’s life. He knew about NFTs and helped me to find the correct way to do it. We asked help from 3 of our friends, unevenpun (developer), Lasalien (Discord builder), and oranJ (video editor), and started a journey where we don’t even know if we can make it happen or not.

I was drawing, trying to get noticed on Twitter, my boyfriend was trying to use our NFT account and we were trying to at least get some money to help us. This is not a lie, we needed money and that’s why I started to make Cupcats. I was already following some people in my real Twitter account because of my boyfriend, and wanted to make some honoraries for them, both because I love them and also I didn’t know what else we could do, I thought maybe they could at least help us with some knowledge because they all looked like great people.

I messaged many people, asking for help about understanding what I should do, only CL returned my messages back then. Then I made my first honorary for CL, that was the biggest interaction I ever got, I got hopeful, finished icebergy, and he didn’t even see the honorary I made for him and I got upset. Trying everyday, replying to many tweets hoping to get some to notice me, drawing at the same time, and my boyfriend wasn’t able to help a lot because he was also working so we could live, I got depressed, and an angry reply under Andre Cronje’s tweet turned into something I could never dream of. Andre Cronje retweeted and tweeted about me and Cupcats many times in an hour, people started to rush into our Discord, a lot of people started to follow me and the Cupcats, I was in shock and didn’t even know what to do. We hugged and started crying with my boyfriend, we were looking into our phones, notifications kept coming, it was a dream moment that couldn’t be real or couldn’t be any better.

And then almost every crypto influencer noticed me, many of them offered help, a lot of people messaged me and offered help, I couldn’t return to everyone because there were hundreds of messages. I still remember that day and I’m still grateful to Andre Cronje and everyone offered help. Without you, I wouldn’t be able to believe in myself and be able to think I could do something people would like.

Now with the community’s help, we were able to launch Cupcats, I got incredible support from many people, especially from Tetranode and many more other known people in crypto. I want to thank all of them from here, all of them have an honorary and if I missed anyone, please reach me out.

Thanks to everyone who supported Cupcats and me, people really saved my life and now I want to give back great things in return. That’s why my boyfriend and me are working everyday to make Cupcats bigger, and I hope we can make it so this doesn’t only stay as people helping because of my story.

Drops for Cupcat Holders

Cupcat holders will enjoy 2 different free claim/airdrop NFTs connected to Cupcats.

We are preparing Cupcat houses with Algerian illustrator Ismail Tiffour, and kittens with Turkish illustrator Yaren Kurt.

Drop 1: Houses for Cupcats

Houses will consist of a lot of things. A theme, a window for cats to look outside, food bowls, cat trees, litter boxes and some other things. This house will also be your home in the Cupcat blockchain game. You will be able to fully interact with everything in your house.

Our artist for Cupcat houses is Algerian artist Ismail Tiffour. He is known for his incredible landscape illustrations, and he will work with us to create great homes for Cupcats.

Drop 2: Kittens

Houses won’t be only for Cupcats, all Cupcat owners will also own a kitten. Kittens will have different traits just like Cupcats, they will not have a cup but they will have a bed (or something like a bed) to sit on.

Sweet kittens will be drawn by Turkish female artist Yaren Kurt. She is a young artist still studying in college, and her expertise is drawing things in a cute way. A perfect match for Cupcats!
